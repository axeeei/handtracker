About Cyber Slinky

The Experience

Cyber Slinky is an interactive web experiment that bridges the gap between physical movement and digital art. Using cutting-edge computer vision technology, we track your hand movements in real-time and transform them into stunning particle effects.

Whether you're creating light paintings, generating digital art, or just exploring the possibilities of gesture-based interaction, Cyber Slinky offers a unique window into the future of human-computer interfaces.
